Dosimetry Partition Model
\( A_{tot} = A_{L} + A_{N} + A_{T} \)
\( L = \large \frac{A_{L}}{A_{total}} \)
\( D_N = 50 \large \frac{A_N}{m_N} \)

\( \Large \frac{T}{N} = \frac{ \frac{A_{T}}{m_{T}} }{ \frac{A_{N}}{m_{N}} } \)
\( \large \frac{T}{N} \frac{D_N}{50} = \frac{A_T}{m_T} \)


Derivation $$ \begin{align} A_{tot} &= A_{L} + A_{N} + A_{T} \\ A_{tot} &= A_{tot}L + A_{N} + A_{T} \\ (1-L) A_{tot} &= A_{N} + A_{T} \\ \frac{1}{m_T m_N} (1-L) A_{tot} &= \frac{A_N}{m_N} \frac{1}{m_T} + \frac{A_T}{m_T} \frac{1}{m_N} \\ \frac{1}{m_T m_N} (1-L) A_{tot} &= \frac{D_N}{50} \frac{1}{m_T} + \frac{T}{N} \frac{D_N}{50} \frac{1}{m_N} \\ (1-L) A_{tot} = m_N \frac{D_N}{50} + \frac{T}{N}(M_T)\frac{D_N}{50} \\ 50 (1-L) A_{tot} = D_N m_N + \frac{T}{N} D_N m_T \\ D_N = \frac{ 50~(1-L)A_{tot} }{ m_N + \frac{T}{N}m_T } \end{align} $$
