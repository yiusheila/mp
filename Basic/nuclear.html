Mean Life (τ) [1] The total number of decay counting from t=0 for a source with A0
 = Area Under Decay Curve $$ \begin{align} Total~\#~of~decay &= \int A~dt \\ &=\int^\infty_0 { A_0 e^{-\lambda t} } dt \\ &=\left[ { -\frac{1}{\lambda} A_0 e^{-\lambda t} } \right]^\infty_0 \\ &= 0-\left( { -\frac{1}{\lambda}A_0 } \right) \\ N_{total}&= \frac{A_0}{\lambda} \\ &=A_0 \tau \end{align} $$
where
τ = mean life
\[ \tau = {1 \over \lambda } = {T_{1/2} \over ln(2)}\]
