Derivation

Compton Scattering

  the angle of the scattered photon 		Pre-requisites
  1. Equivalent Mass of Photon
    2.
    Given that, \( E = mc^2 \) and \( E_{photon} = hf \),
    we can express the equivalent mass of photon as \[ m_{eq}c^2=hf \] \[ m_{eq} = \frac{hf}{c^2} \]

  2. Momentum of Photon
    3.
    $$ \begin{align} p_{photon} &= m_{eq}c \\ &= \left( \frac{hf}{c^2} \right) c \\ &= \frac{hf}{c} \end{align} $$ ∴ For Photon,
    \[ pc=hf \]

  3. Relativistic Energy-Momentum Relation
    4.
    \[ E^2 = (pc)^2 + (m_0c^2)^2 \]


Derivation of the Direction of Scattered Photon
  1. Conservation of Momentum
    2.
    \[ \large \vec{p_i} + \vec{p_e} = \vec{p_s} + \vec{p_{e'}} \] \( \large \because \vec{e}=0 \) \[ \large \vec{p_{e'}} = \vec{p_i} - \vec{p_s} \] $$ \begin{align} & \quad \large ~|\vec{p_{e'}}|^2 \\ &= \large \vec{p_{e'}} \cdot \vec{p_{e'}} \\ &= \large (\vec{p_i} - \vec{p_s}) \cdot (\vec{p_i} - \vec{p_s}) \\ &= \large |\vec{p_i}|^2 -2\vec{p_i}\vec{p_s} + |\vec{p_s}|^2 \\ {p_{e'}}^2 &= {p_i}^2 + {p_s}^2 -2{p_i}{p_s}cos\theta \\ \end{align} $$ Multiply both side with \(c^2\), \[ {p_{e'}}^2c^2 = {p_i}^2 c^2 + {p_s}^2 c^2 -2{p_i}{p_s}c^2cos\theta \] Replacing \(pc=hf\) for incoming and scattered photons, \[ {p_{e'}}^2c^2 = (hf_i)^2 + (hf_s)^2 -2 ({hf_i}) ({hf_s}) cos\theta ~~ ................. (1) \]
  2. Conservation of Energy
    3.
    \[ hf_i + m_e c^2 = hf_s + \sqrt{ (p_{e'}c)^2 + (m_e c^2)^2 } \] \[ hf_i - hf_s + m_e c^2 = \sqrt{ (p_{e'}c)^2 + (m_e c^2)^2 } \] \[ ( hf_i - hf_s + m_e c^2 )^2 = (p_{e'}c)^2 + (m_e c^2)^2 \] \[ {p_{e'}}^2 c^2 = ( hf_i - hf_s + m_e c^2 )^2 - (m_e c^2)^2 ~~ ................. (2) \]
  3. Equate (1) and (2),
    4. $$ \begin{align} (hf_i)^2 + (hf_s)^2 -2 ({hf_i}) ({hf_s}) cos\theta &= ( hf_i - hf_s + m_e c^2 )^2 - (m_e c^2)^2 \\ &= \left[~(hf_i - hf_s) + m_e c^2~\right]^2 - (m_e c^2)^2 \\ &= (hf_i - hf_s)^2 + 2(hf_i - hf_s)(m_e c^2) + (m_e c^2)^2 - (m_e c^2)^2 \\ &= (hf_i)^2 -2(hf_i)(hf_s) + (hf_s)^2 + 2(hf_i - hf_s)(m_e c^2) \\ -2 ({hf_i}) ({hf_s}) cos\theta &= -2(hf_i)(hf_s) + 2(hf_i - hf_s)(m_e c^2) \\ -cos\theta &= -1 + \frac{2h(f_i-f_s)(m_ec^2)}{2h^2f_if_s} \\ 1 - cos\theta &= \frac{(f_i-f_s)(m_ec^2)}{hf_if_s} \\ \frac{h}{m_e c}(1- cos\theta) &= \frac{c}{f_s} - \frac{c}{f_i} \end{align} $$
