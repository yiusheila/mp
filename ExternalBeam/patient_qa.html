Tolerance / Action Level
Tolerance limit
 =  plan + treatment operating normally
 =  only random error

Action limit
   =  no risking harm to patient
Gamma Index \[ \Gamma^2 = \left( \frac{\Delta D}{\Delta D_c} \right)^2 + \left( \frac{\Delta DTA}{\Delta DTA_c} \right)^2 \]
where
DTA = distance to agreement
ΔDc = acceptance criteria of dose difference
ΔDTAc = acceptance criteria of DTA
Γ > 1  →  Fail

Dose Measurement
1.  Point Dose
  • cylindrical ionisation chamber in phantom
  • small size
    max/min across the IC volume < 5% of mean chamber dose
  • should place at region corresponding to PTV/OAR
  • should be compared to the active volume of the chamber on planned dose distribution

2.  2D Dose Distribution
  • using IC arrays / diode arrays / EPID / film

3.  Reconstruction Method
  • Forward Calculation Algorithm
    measurement data with EPID / 2D diode arrays / 2D IC arrays
    →  act as
    fluence map
    → feed to algorithm to calculate the dose deposit in patient CT
  • Rotating 2D Array in Phantom
    →  rotate with gantry to stay perpendicular to the beam
    →  reconstruct 3D dose distribution in phantom
  • During Treatment Exit Dose Reconstruction
    →  EPID collect transmission flence map
    →  correct for EPID response
    →  backproject dose deposit in patient
