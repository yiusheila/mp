Preprocessing
  1. Unbiased Noise
  2. Prewhitening
  3. Cross Correlation
    -----------------
    R(tau)=integral(x(t)y(t+tau))dt
    tau = time delay

    - if x=y, R=Autocorrelation
    - unlike convolution which flip one of the function

    Autocorrelation
    = time series integrate itself at difference time
