TLD Reading Preheat before reading
  1. Place TLD in oven
  2. Open Thermosoft
  3. Profile > Open > TLD-100 PREHEAT
  4. (on oven)
    HEATING ON
    HEATING START
    (on software)
    Press start button

Read TLD
  1. Open RADOS
  2. Set-up > Saving of results >
    Target
    > C:\TLD\(file_name)_Stability.txt
  3. Turn on gas

    4. Run dummy to check reader
  4. Put in dummy TLD in slot
  5. Run Local button
    > choose 100 or 100H
    >
    OK
  6. Record the N2 °C, N2 flow, PMT °C, Dark counts, LED k-counts and vacuum level

    7. Read TLD
  7. Set-up > Saving of results >
    Target
    > C:\TLD\(file_name).txt
  8. Put in TLD in slot
  9. Run Local button
    > choose 100 or 100H
    >
    OK
