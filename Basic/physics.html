\[ F=\frac{mv^2}{r} \]

v = tangential speed

Charge
\[ \textrm{For} \quad Charge~Density~\lambda = {dQ \over dh} \] \[ Electrical~Flux~\Phi = k\lambda h \] $$ \begin{align} Electrical~Force~F & = {\Phi \over A} \\ & = {k\lambda h \over 2\pi r h} \\ & = \left( {k \over 2\pi} \right) {1 \over r} \lambda \end{align} $$
