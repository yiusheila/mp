\[ \large D(t) = \frac{34.6 ~\Gamma ~T_p ~A_0 ~(1-e^{-\tau t}) }{r^2} \]
\( 34.6 = \large \frac{24~[hr/day]}{ln(2)} \)
\(\Gamma\) = gamma ray constant
\( A_0 \) = activity at the time of release
\( T_p \) = physical half life
\( r \) = distance from source
When \( t = \infty \),
\[ \large D(t) = \frac{34.6 ~\Gamma ~T_p ~A_0 ~O }{r^2} \]
\( O \) = occupancy factor
