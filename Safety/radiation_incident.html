Radiation Incident

INES (International Nuclear and Radiological Event Scale)
Classification Criteria
  1. People and the Environment
    • radiation doses to people
    • quantities of radioactive materials that spread from a facility

  2. Radiological Barriers and Control
    • significant quantities of radioactive materials CONFINED within a facility

  3. Defence-in-Depth
      = to weaken the attack
    • safety measures did not function properly
