Installation
  1. water cooling at the back
  2. install base frame (under turn table)
    level
    distance from finishing floor ← the patient couch place onto base frame
  3. 4 Piece Breakdown
    1st piece: Stand
    2nd piece: Gantry
    3rd piece: Patient Couch
    4th piece: Counterbalance

    (pin from stand need to release to lock the gantry head before servicing the gantry)
  4. join the stand with the gantry on jig cart first
  5. bolt down the stand to the floor
  6. turn the gantry to 90°
    add counterbalance to the other side of gantry head
  7. Connect the water hoses and wires
    (components in gantry head → radiation resistant)
  8. Preliminarily check the level of the stand
  9. Install couch onto turn table
    connect cables

  10. Isocentre Adjustment
    * assuming collimator bearing is optimal
    1.  adjust the "iso-turner" disc to sit in line with the collimation centre
       3 pin to adjust rotation
       4 pin to adjust translation
    2.  adjust gantry sagging + translation
       (since turntable cannot adjust translation)
       (turn the couch to 90° and 270° → find the couch axis)
       (translate the stand to match the couch axis)
       take measurement when gantry @ 0° and 180°
       adjust the gantry level by adding shims to the bottom of the stand
    3.  adjust couch
    4.  install back pointer
    5.  radiation isocentre using "iso-lock"
       ~ to wintson luts
    6.  use front pointer to find the mechanical isocentre
    7.  head leakage
