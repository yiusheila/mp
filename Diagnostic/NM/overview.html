Mechanism
  1. Tracer undergoes β+ decay
  2. Positron emitted
  3. Positron and Electron Annihilate
  4. Two opposite direction γ
    with 511 keV emitted simultaneously
  = Rotating Gamma Camera
Advantage High Sensitivity
  • Coincidence-detection of opposite 511 keV photon
  • Do not require collimator
 Cost Effective
  • Tracers normally have longer half-life
      (transport friendly)
Disadvantage Short Half-Life
  • usually produced with on-site cyclotron
 Depends on Collimator
  • collimator blocks incoming γ
  • reduced sensitivity
